ABOUT US

Vision • Mission • Values

Our Vision

To see thriving communities in Uganda and beyond, where every individual—child, adult, and youth—has access to tools, education, and opportunities that unlock their full potential.

Our Mission

Through KidSpark educational games, adult literacy programmes, and youth sport initiatives, we deliver sustainable learning experiences, build life skills, and foster leadership to transform lives.

Our Values

  • Integrity: We act with honesty, transparency, and accountability.
  • Inclusivity: We ensure no one is left behind, embracing diversity and equity.
  • Innovation: We leverage creative, tech‐driven solutions to tackle local challenges.
  • Collaboration: We partner with communities, schools, and organizations for lasting impact.

About The Next Generation (TNG)

The Next Generation (TNG) is a youth-led, community-based organization dedicated to transforming lives through education, empowerment, and inclusion. Originating in the Kyangwali Refugee Settlement, we work to bridge educational and social gaps in vulnerable communities.

Our programs are designed to uplift individuals at every stage of life—children, youth, and adults—through initiatives like KidSpark for playful learning, adult literacy for functional skills, and soccer-based youth mentoring. We focus on building a generation equipped with knowledge, character, and leadership.

At TNG, we believe in a future where every person, regardless of background or circumstance, has the opportunity to thrive, lead, and contribute meaningfully to their community.
