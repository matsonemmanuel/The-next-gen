Literacy Program

The Next Generation (TNG) is committed to empowering communities through education. Our Adult Literacy Program targets adults who missed formal education opportunities, helping them gain essential reading, writing, and numeracy skills.

Since its inception in 2022, the program has reached over 950 adults across different zones of the Kyangwali refugee settlement and surrounding areas. With the help of 30+ trained volunteer instructors, we've conducted 120+ class sessions and witnessed significant growth in learners’ confidence and independence.

Impact Highlights (2022 - Present):

  • 📚 950+ adults enrolled in literacy programs
  • 👩‍🏫 30+ dedicated volunteer instructors
  • 🏫 6 community learning centers established
  • 🗓️ 120+ literacy sessions conducted
  • 🎯 80% average improvement in literacy assessment scores

The journey is ongoing, and with continued support, we aim to scale this program to reach 1,500 adults by the end of 2025.
