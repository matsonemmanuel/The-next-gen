KidSpark: Igniting Young Minds Through Play

KidSpark is a transformative initiative by The Next Generation (TNG) designed to integrate interactive, play-based learning into primary education for children in refugee and underserved communities.

Through culturally relevant games, KidSpark enhances literacy, numeracy, and creativity while making learning enjoyable. The project encourages participation and curiosity, creating a strong foundation for lifelong learning.

Currently implemented in schools within the Kyangwali refugee settlement, KidSpark is powered by community volunteers and dedicated educators who are passionate about shaping brighter futures.

  • Age Group: 6 – 12 years
  • Focus: Foundational skills & creative thinking
  • Reach: Kyangwali Refugee Settlement
  • Supported By: TNG educators & local volunteers
